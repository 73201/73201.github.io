Welcome, this website is all about Morrocan food.

You can choose between normal or vegan food. If you don't wan't to eat, you can make yourself or other people a drink, I would personally prefer to make Mint Tea, the taste is so good!

On the menu Food you can find recipes off Moroccan food.

On the menu Drinks you can find recipes off Moroccan drinks.

On the menu Contact you can find some things about me, as example my number and gmail.

I am Moroccan myself and I really like the food. Food is a part of culture in every country. I would like to show other people a piece of our culture with this website :)

I would like it if you leave a review with photos, and whether it worked out well or not. Also let me know if you liked it or not.

I hope you enjoy the baking and cooking.

MOROCCAN FOOD MEANING TRIBE
HARIRA SOUP A MOROCCAN SOUP MADE FROM A VARIETY OF VEGETABLES WITH LENTILS, CHICKPEAS, AND CORIANDER. ARABIC
CHEBAKIA A MOROCCAN PASTRY MADE OF STRIPS OF DOUGH ROLLED TO RESEMBLE A ROSE, DEEP-FRIED UNTIL GOLDEN, THEN COATED WITH A SYRUP MADE OF HONEY AND ORANGE BLOSSOM WATER AND SPRINKLED WITH SESAME. ARABIC
MOROCCAN VEGETABLESTEW A MOROCCAN STEW IS SLOW-COOCKED SAVORY STEW, TYPICALLY MADE WITH VEGETABLES OR FRUIT. SPICES, NUTS, AND DRIED FRUITS ARE ALSO USED. SPICES USED ARE GINGER, CUMIN, TURMERIC, CINNAMON, AND SAFFRON. AMAZIGH
ARTICHOKE TAGINE MOROCCQN TAGINE IS SLOW-COOCKED SAVORY STEWS, TYPICALLY MADE WITH SLICED MEAT, POULTRY OR FISH TOGETHER WITH VEGETABLES OR FRUIT. AMAZIGH
MOROCCAN MINT TEA MORE THAN JUST A HOT DRINK, MOROCCO'S FAMOUS MINT TEA IS A SYMBOL OF MOROCCAN HOSPITALITTY AND CULTURE. ARABIC/AMAZIGH
MOROCCAN SPICED COFFEE A FRAGRANT BIEND OF DARK COFFEE AN WARM SPICES. AMAZIGH
ORANGE BLOSSOM AND MINT LEMONADE A FRESH AND TASTY DRINK TO COOL DOWN IN THE HOT DAYS. ARABIC/AMAZIGH
